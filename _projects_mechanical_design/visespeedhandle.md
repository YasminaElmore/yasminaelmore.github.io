Designed and optimized a custom vise speed handle by leveraging shape optimization and generative design to reduce mass while maintaining structural integrity, ensuring manufacturability and performance under real-world loading conditions.

 <div class="section">
    <h2>Roles Played</h2>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Mechanical Designer:</strong> Developed 3D CAD models ensuring compatibility with tightening and loosening mechanisms.</li>
        <li><strong>FEA Analyst:</strong> Conducted Finite Element Analysis (FEA) to evaluate stress distribution, displacement, and load resistance.</li>
        <li><strong>Optimization Engineer:</strong> Applied shape optimization and generative design for mass reduction while maintaining mechanical integrity.</li>
        <li><strong>Material Researcher:</strong> Selected materials (Aluminum 6061, Tough 2000, Aluminum AlSi10Mg) based on mechanical properties and manufacturability.</li>
    </ul>
</div>
    
<div class="section">
    <h2>Skills Developed</h2>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Finite Element Analysis (FEA):</strong> Modeled load distribution, stress concentrations, and displacement effects under various force applications.</li>
        <li><strong>Shape Optimization & Generative Design:</strong> Compared traditional and AI-driven optimization for structural efficiency and mass reduction.</li>
        <li><strong>Structural Mechanics & Load Analysis:</strong> Evaluated applied forces, constraints, and bending behavior to prevent failure.</li>
        <li><strong>CAD Modeling & Design for Manufacturing:</strong> Created parametric models in SolidWorks/Fusion 360 ensuring manufacturability.</li>
        <li><strong>Material Selection & Failure Analysis:</strong> Chose materials based on yield strength, stress limits, and fabrication feasibility.</li>
    </ul>
</div>
